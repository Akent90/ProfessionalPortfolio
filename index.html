About Me

Hello! My name is Alex. Form ever follows function has taught me to appreciate the elegance of simplicity. This appreciation for straightforward, effective solutions is the cornerstone to my approach for both life and work.

The first exposure to programming in my career came from my time in the Army, working with Linux-based command and control software for radars. Initializing local networks and programming hard drives for radars completely solidified my love for functional utility. The software's reliability, despite its lack of aesthetic frills, has profoundly influenced my engineering ethos. I am driven to integrate this practical reliability with the streamlined efficiency I've cultivated throughout my life.
